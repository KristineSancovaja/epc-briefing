Consultation March 2026

EPC 2026 Public Consultation on SCT Inst Scheme Rulebook Change Requests

The EPC opened a public consultation on 2026 change requests for the SCT Inst rulebook, including proposals to extend name field character length from 70 to 140 characters and to migrate EPC payment scheme rulebooks to the latest ISO 20022 version. The consultation will inform a September 2026 EPC decision on whether 2029 rulebooks migrate to a newer ISO 20022 version (potentially the 2027 ISO version) via a big-bang changeover.

Rulebook October 2025

Important Update About the 2025 EPC Payment Scheme Rulebooks (Version 1.1)

The EPC released version 1.1 of all five 2025 payment scheme rulebooks, effective 5 October 2025. The key update revises the deadline for phasing out unstructured address formats to 15 November 2026 (originally 22 November 2026), aligning with the November 2026 Swift Standards MX Release.

EPC Recommendation on ISO 20022 Customer Reporting of SCTs, OCT Inst and SDDs (Version 5.0)

Version 5.0 of the EPC customer reporting recommendation was released, providing mapping of EPC payment scheme rulebook requirements to ISO 20022 reporting messages (camt.052, camt.053, camt.054). This version includes coverage for the One-Leg Out Instant Credit Transfer (OCT Inst) scheme and took effect 5 October 2025.

Regulation October 2025

SEPA Instant Credit Transfer (SCT Inst) Rulebook Version 1.1 - IPR Compliance

The 2025 SCT Inst rulebook version 1.1 is fully compliant with EU Regulation 2024/886 (Instant Payments Regulation), amending the SEPA Regulation. The rulebook is based on the 2019 message version of ISO 20022 and is effective until 21 November 2027. Key changes include mandatory reachability requirements, hybrid address format support, and millisecond timestamps for instant transfers.
