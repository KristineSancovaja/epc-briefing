2025 EPC Payment Scheme Rulebooks Version 1.1 - Important Update

The EPC released version 1.1 of all five 2025 payment scheme rulebooks (SCT, SCT Inst, SDD Core, SDD B2B, OCT Inst), effective 5 October 2025. Technical June 2025

ECB ISO 20022 Message Unfreeze Strategy Status Update

The ECB outlined its ISO 20022 message unfreeze strategy: implementing ISO Maintenance Release 2024 in June 2026 and annual real-time maintenance starting November 2026. TIPS core payment messages under EPC governance will remain on ISO MR2019 aligned with international market practice.

Regulation October 2025

November 2026 Deadline: Mandatory Structured Address Formats in EPC Payment Messages

As of 15 November 2026, unstructured address formats will no longer be permitted in EPC payment messages. Only fully structured or hybrid address formats will be accepted, with Town Name and Country as minimum required structured elements.
